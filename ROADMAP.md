## Working guardrails (current)
- **Reversibility gate** (adopted 6/01): do anything reversible ‚Äî including backed-up prod data writes; **always snapshot before a prod mutation.** Queue genuine one-way doors (force-push/history rewrite, un-backed-up destructive SQL, bulk ops, real-user emails, infra teardown, auth-config lockout) for an attended, approved moment.
